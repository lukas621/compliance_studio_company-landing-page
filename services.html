Accessibility

An 18-course series covering the full scope of accessibility obligation in clinical and administrative settings. From the admitting conversation to discharge planning, every course is mapped to AODA, Accreditation Canada Qmentum standards, and CNO continuing-competence requirements.

Indigenous Cultural Safety

Cultural safety grounded in the Truth and Reconciliation Commission Calls to Action (23 and 24), UNDRIP, and the specific obligations of Canadian healthcare institutions. Developed in partnership with Indigenous advisors and communities — not authored about Indigenous peoples, but with them.

After ten years, none of the seven health-related TRC Calls to Action have been fully implemented. Only 44% of Canadian nursing schools offer mandatory Indigenous health courses. This training area exists because the gap is still open.

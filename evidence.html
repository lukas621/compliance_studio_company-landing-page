The audit packet

A single PDF. Submission-formatted. On demand.

Generated from the live evidence model across any of MECC's six training areas. Every page carries the institutional seal, the surveyor reference number, and the name of the clinical SME who validated the underlying scenarios.

  • 02 Completion roster — role-indexed, dated
  • 03 Decision-quality summary — per module, per role
  • 04 SME validation letter — named, signed, dated
  • 05 Accreditation alignment matrix
  • 06 Continuing-competence attestation (where applicable)

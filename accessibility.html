Design decisions

What we decided. Why we decided it.

  • 01
    17px body type, 1.6 line-height. Above the 16px AAA readability floor. Readers with low vision can enlarge to 200% with no layout reflow.
  • 02
    No autoplay, no motion without consent. The site honours prefers-reduced-motion. All transitions degrade to cross-fades at the system level.
  • 03
    Keyboard-first focus. Every interactive element has a visible focus ring. Tab order matches visual order. No focus traps except on modal dialogs, and those are labelled.
  • 04
    No colour alone. Every state conveyed by colour is also conveyed by label, shape, or position. If you remove colour, the hierarchy still reads.
  • 05
    No sensory-only CAPTCHA, ever. Forms use invisible anti-spam heuristics, never puzzles a user must see or hear.
  • 06
    Plain-language pricing. Every tier is named, explained, and priced in plain language — not in a marketing euphemism.

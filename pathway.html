N° 02 · The Pathway

From diagnostic to institutional standard. One pathway.

Every engagement begins with understanding. Each step produces evidence. No tier is purchased — each is earned, through outcomes measured at the one before it.

Five tiers Discovery → Pilot → Essentials → Strategic → Enterprise
Tier 01 — Entry

Discovery Sprint

Duration
3–4 weeks
Investment
Complimentary · 3–4 weeks

What you receive

  • Capability Diagnostic Report (institutional, department-indexed)
  • Executive Briefing — 90 minutes, board-readable
  • Three-Year Capability Roadmap
  • Content Preview Kit built from your own policy

What you prove

Where your institution stands — and where the gaps are. A named, documented picture of accessibility capability against your written policy.

Who it's for

CNOs and VPs who need a data-driven capability assessment before committing to a pilot or a license. Discovery is complimentary — the institution commits nothing until Pilot.

◆ Diagnostic Complete
Tier 02 — Validation

Pilot License

Duration
90–180 days
Investment
From $45,000 CAD

Founding cohort · First two institutions enter as Design Partners at a reduced fee, with published case-study rights.

What you receive

  • 15 modules across 1 role pathway
  • Facilitator kits for in-service sessions
  • Full LMS integration and SSO handshake
  • Success measurement, pre- and post- delta
  • Quarterly business review with senior partner

What you prove

Measurable capability improvement in one department, under real operating conditions, with a pre- and post- decision-quality delta documented as published case-study material.

Who it's for

Institutions that need proof — at the department level — before committing to an enterprise license.

◆ Pilot Validated
Recommended entry point · Designed as the institutional foundation tier

Term
Annual

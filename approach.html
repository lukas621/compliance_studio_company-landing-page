How we build

Five steps. Every programme.

Every module starts with the institution's own policy documents and regulatory obligations. Content is drafted, reviewed by named subject-matter experts across clinical, disability, and cultural domains, and validated against accreditation standards before it reaches a learner.

AI augments the pipeline — drafting, regulatory updates, consistency. It does not replace the expert, the editor, or the institutional review.

01Obligation mapping

Policy and regulation mapped to roles, settings, and decision moments.

02Scenario authoring

Instructional designers with domain expertise author every scenario.

03Expert validation

Every scenario validated by named clinical, disability, and cultural reviewers. Auditable.

04LMS packaging

SCORM/xAPI packaged for the institution's LMS. Integration tested before deployment.

05Evidence architecture

Assessment and reporting designed to produce audit-ready evidence of capability, not completion checkmarks.

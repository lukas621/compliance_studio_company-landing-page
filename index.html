The gap

Healthcare institutions have compliance policies. Most do not yet have compliance capability.

A written policy is not a trained workforce. A mandatory training completion is not a measured capability. When a surveyor asks how your institution operationalizes its obligations — in accessibility, equity, cultural safety, trauma-informed care — the answer is rarely on the policy shelf.

Ten years after the TRC, none of the seven health-related Calls to Action have been fully implemented. Only 44% of Canadian nursing schools offer mandatory Indigenous health courses. Twenty-nine percent of physiotherapy students report receiving zero 2SLGBTQ+ training. The gap is not unique to any one domain.

MECC licenses the training infrastructure that closes it. Six training areas. Two product tools. Evidence that satisfies the surveyor, the board, and the regulator.
